HR Attrition Analysis (Excel Project)

Overview:
This project analyzes employee attrition trends using HR data. It explores factors such as age, department, job role, salary, and satisfaction levels to uncover turnover patterns. The goal is to provide actionable insights that help HR teams improve retention strategies.

Dataset:
File: HRattrition.xlsx
Contents: Employee demographics, job roles, compensation, satisfaction scores, and attrition status.
Purpose: To identify drivers of employee turnover and support HR decision-making.

Objectives:
- Detect key factors influencing attrition
- Visualize patterns across departments, roles, and demographics
- Provide recommendations for workforce retention

Tools & Techniques:
- Excel Functions: Data cleaning, formulas, conditional formatting
- Pivot Tables: Summarizing attrition by department, role, and demographics
- Charts & Graphs: Attrition trends visualized with bar charts, pie charts, and line graphs
- Dashboards: Interactive Excel dashboard for HR insights

Key Insights (to be filled after analysis):
- Example: Higher attrition in Sales department
- Example: Employees with low satisfaction show higher turnover
- Example: Younger employees tend to leave within 2 years

Usage:
1. Open HRattrition.xlsx in Excel
2. Explore pivot tables and charts for insights
3. Review dashboard for HR strategy recommendations
